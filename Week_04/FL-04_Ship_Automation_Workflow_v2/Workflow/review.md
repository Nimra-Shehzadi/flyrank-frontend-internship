# Workflow Review

## Time Saved

Manual Process:
- Research: 20 minutes
- Project Description: 15 minutes
- Features List: 10 minutes
- README: 20 minutes
- LinkedIn Post: 15 minutes

Total Manual Time:
Approximately 80 minutes

Workflow Time:
Approximately 15–20 minutes

Estimated Time Saved:
Around 60 minutes per project.

---

## Failure Points

- AI may generate inaccurate technical details.
- README formatting may require adjustments.
- Generated features may not perfectly match project scope.
- LinkedIn content may need tone improvements.
- Outputs should always be reviewed before publishing.

---

## Human Review Required

A human should verify:

- Technical accuracy
- Grammar and spelling
- Formatting
- Project-specific details
- Final publishing quality

Human review is required before using any AI-generated content publicly.
